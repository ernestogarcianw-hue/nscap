About North Star Capital

North Star Capital provides fast, common-sense hard money financing to experienced real estate investors, landlords, and developers nationwide. We specialize in asset-based lending for fix-and-flip, BRRRR, rental acquisitions, and ground-up construction projects.

Who We Serve

We work with active investors who understand their markets and have a clear exit strategy. Whether you're flipping single-family homes, building small multifamily projects, or scaling a rental portfolio, we focus on the deal fundamentals: property value, location, borrower experience, and viable exit plans.

Our Approach

We underwrite to the asset, not arbitrary credit boxes. If the numbers make sense and you have a track record, we can move quickly. Most term sheets go out within 24 hours, and we can close in as little as 5–10 business days when needed.

Markets

We lend nationwide with deep experience in the Southwest, Midwest, and select growth markets. We avoid highly speculative land plays and focus on properties with clear comps and realistic valuations.
